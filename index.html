Who I am... Why I run

A great Democrat, Adlai Stevenson, once famously quipped: “In America, anybody can grow up to be president.

…it’s just one of the risks we take.”

A s we all know, sadly, Mr. Stevenson did not grow up to be president…but his warning is well-taken. There comes a time when each of us who values living in a democracy has to ask him or herself, could I do better? …do more?

If and when your honest and sincere answer to that question is “yes,” then you simply must try to do so. It’s a civic obligation and a matter of social responsibility.

I ask you for your support

I ask you for your support as I seek to do better, do more as a member of the Scott County Board of Supervisors.

With your help, I plan to run a very aggressive campaign and over the coming months, you’ll hear lots of campaign-style sound bites tossed about. Some will be helpful, most will not. But, you deserve better.
